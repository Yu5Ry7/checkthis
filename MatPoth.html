Day 1

Flower Garden

Start your day by visiting the Flower Garden, immersing yourself in the beauty and fragrance of various flowers

Photopoint

Afterward, head to Photopoint and capture stunning photographs of the scenic surroundings.

Elephant Park

experience the Carmelagiri Elephant Safari and enjoy a thrilling ride while observing elephants in their natural habitat

Botanical Garden

visit the Botanical Garden and take a leisurely stroll through the lush greenery, appreciating the diverse plant species.

Mattupetty Tea Factory

Explore the Mattupetty Tea Factory, where you can learn about tea production and enjoy a fresh cup of tea.

Mattupetty Dam

Proceeding to the Mattupetty Dam and indulge in speed boating activities on the reservoir.

Film Shooting Point

Wrap up the day by visiting the Film Shooting Point Elephant Arrival Spot, known for its picturesque landscapes and occasional elephant sightings.

Top Station

Finally, heading to Top Station to enjoy a breathtaking view of the Tamil Nadu/Kerala border.

Day 2

Visiting Tea Plantation

Starting early in the morning to explore the beautiful Tea Plantations and learn about tea production.

Virvara Waterfalls

Enjoy the breathtaking view of Virvara Waterfalls and spend some time in its serene surroundings.

Tiger Cave

Visit Tiger Cave and admire the natural rock formations and scenic landscapes.

Spice Garden

Experience the aromatic Spice Garden and learn about various spices and their uses.

Elephant Safari

Embark on an exciting Elephant Safari and get up close with these majestic creatures.

Zipline adventure

Get your adrenaline pumping with a thrilling Zipline adventure.

Adventure Park

Visiting Adventure Park Dreamland or Wonder Valley for more adventurous activities and fun.

Pothamed Viewpoint

Heading to Pothamed Viewpoint to witness a mesmerizing sunset and enjoy the panoramic views.

Hydel Park

Conclude the day by exploring Hydel Park and unwind in its peaceful ambiance.
