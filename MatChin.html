Day 1

Flower Garden

Start your day by visiting the Flower Garden, immersing yourself in the beauty and fragrance of various flowers

Photopoint

Afterward, head to Photopoint and capture stunning photographs of the scenic surroundings.

Elephant Park

experience the Carmelagiri Elephant Safari and enjoy a thrilling ride while observing elephants in their natural habitat

Botanical Garden

visit the Botanical Garden and take a leisurely stroll through the lush greenery, appreciating the diverse plant species.

Mattupetty Tea Factory

Explore the Mattupetty Tea Factory, where you can learn about tea production and enjoy a fresh cup of tea.

Mattupetty Dam

Proceeding to the Mattupetty Dam and indulge in speed boating activities on the reservoir.

Film Shooting Point

Wrap up the day by visiting the Film Shooting Point Elephant Arrival Spot, known for its picturesque landscapes and occasional elephant sightings.

Top Station

Finally, heading to Top Station to enjoy a breathtaking view of the Tamil Nadu/Kerala border.

Day 2

Eravikulam National Park

Starting early in the morning and visit Eravikulam National Park to explore the wildlife and scenic landscapes.

Tea plantations

Proceeding to the tea plantations and take a guided tour to learn about tea production and enjoy the beautiful surroundings

Lakkom Waterfalls

visiting Lakkom Waterfalls and spend some time enjoying the natural beauty and tranquility.

Sandalwood Forest

Heading to Marayoor Sandalwood Forest to explore the forest and learn about the cultivation of sandalwood.

Jaggery Production Unit

Visit a jaggery production unit and witness the traditional process of making jaggery.

Chinnar Wildlife Sanctuary

Explore Chinnar Wildlife Sanctuary, spot wildlife, and immerse yourself in nature

Thoovan Waterfalls

Visiting Thoovan Waterfalls and marvel at the cascading water amidst picturesque surroundings.

Jeep safari

Embarking on a thrilling jeep safari in Kaanthaloor and explore the scenic landscapes.

Muni Ara

Driving to Muni Ara and spend some time in this serene location known for its tranquility.

Erachilpara Waterfalls

Visiting Erachilpara Waterfalls and enjoy the breathtaking views of the cascading falls.

Fruits orchard

Visiting fruits thotam (fruit orchard) and indulge in the flavors of fresh fruits.

Shooting Point

Make a quick stop at the Shooting Point to capture some stunning photographs..

Brinthavanam Waterfalls

Then visiting Brinthavanam Waterfalls and enjoying the natural beauty of the falls.

Otha Maram

If time and energy permit, embark on an off-road adventure to Otha Maram, known for its rugged terrain and scenic views.
